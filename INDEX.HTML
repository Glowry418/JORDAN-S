About JORDAN

EMUZE JORDAN SAMUEL well known as "Jordan" (born March 21,1994) is a Passionate Lover of God.a Nigerian singer, Praise Machine and a Powerful Worship Leader.

He hails from Edo State. Jordan's Vision is towards bringing the light of the Kingdom into the Sphere of Every Man

Likes and Dislikes

Jordan is a man who is passionate about his faith and loves to sing gospel music.

He enjoys spending time with his friends and family, especially when they share his love for music.

Jordan is also an avid reader and enjoys books that explore spiritual themes. In his free time, he likes to listen to some music and Chat with his friends.

Jordan dislikes dishonesty and people who are not genuine. He also has a strong aversion to spicy foods.
